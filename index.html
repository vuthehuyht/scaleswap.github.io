Integrating Reward Staking With Gameplay

Revolve Games is revolutionizing the way DeFi staking is integrated with blockchain gaming. The staking rewards of a given tethered actionable NFT are dependant on the in-game performance of that asset; be it a character, vehicle or game builder. As such, players are highly incentivized to participate in gaming and level up their NFT. Liquidity stakers are also incentivized to perform better against their opponents with increased staking multipliers.

Metaverse

Explore The Galaxy

The Revolve Metaverse is an inter-planetary sandbox. Explore the galaxy with your NFT tethered spacecraft, buy land on planets, shape the landscape and build your own games. Teleportation portal NFTs allow you to instantly travel between your favourite set desinations in the metaverse.

Buy & Rent Land

The metaverse is composed of a limited number of planets and limited blocks of land on each planet. Land can be purchased, resold or rented out. In a rental contract, the renter stakes a set amount of RPG, with the staking rewards going to the land owner.

Build Games

Mint RPG tethered game builder asset packs and build games on top of your purchased or rented land. The more popular your game is with the community, the better the staking rewards will be.

Dynamic Economy

The Revolve metaverse is built on a robust economy, with a combination of ownable assets and assets tethered to RPG stakes. Players are incentivized to stake their RPG tokens in order to be able to enjoy the full gaming experience in the Revolve metaverse.

Game Builder Asset Packs SDK

An SDK will allow whitelisted third-party developers to create their own game builder asset packs, which will be available to users on the platform. Developers will be able to monetize their game builder packs.

SDK for Metaverse Development

Third-party developers will be able to develop entire parallel metaverses which will exist within the great Revolve Games multiverse.
