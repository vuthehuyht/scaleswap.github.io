The New NFT Protocol That’s Sparking a DeFi Revolution

Charged Particles is the groundbreaking new protocol that lets you put digital assets inside your NFTs. Now, ordinary NFTs (think neutral molecules) can contain a digital "charge" inside — ERC20, ERC721 or ERC1155 — giving you the unprecedented power to create nested NFTs. If you can digitize it, you can deposit it into your NFTs.

For Artists

01
What are the benefits for an artist?

Nested NFTs allow for multiple artists to collaborate on a collection, include work-in-progress images, maintain engagement with their collectors over time, deliver content gradually, distribute social tokens or other community rewards, and so much more.

02
How can this help me as an artist?

Having another tool at your disposal means you can tell your story in a new way.

03
What happens if my artwork doesn't sell?

Leveraging your NFT as a savings account while a piece waits to find it’s owner means lossless gains and a rising price-floor over time.

DeFi

01
What in the world is a DeFi NFT?

An NFT that is interest bearing. With DeFi NFTs we are transforming static NFTs into robust financial vehicles; recreating the traditional finance ecosystem on the blockchain. Along with the time-locking mechanisms we enable the creation of trust funds, retirement funds, college funds, estate planning, and more.

02
How can my NFT earn interest?

We are fully integrated with Aave for DeFi Asset support; meaning NFTs can earn yield that is programmable and can be directed wherever you’d like. Any token that is supported by Aave can be earning interest inside your NFT.

03
How does this compare to traditional finance?

Now NFTs can be transformed into funds that hold other digital assets, including any erc-20 and other NFTs (similar to investment funds, hedge funds, etc); and fractionalized (similar to index funds).

Use Case 01

Interest-Bearing NFTs

  • Deposit tokens supported by Aave that then get converted to aTokens by the protocol into an NFT to create yield-bearing assets.
    ‍
  • The interest generated from this asset is programmable, which means you have control over the principle and the interest earned; send it, keep it, save it.
    ‍
  • You have the option to time-lock the interest-bearing tokens and come back to it in a year, or another designated amount of time to collect your cumulative gains.
Learn More

Use Case 02

Nested NFTs

  • Create a brand new asset class known as a Nested NFT. Bring to life a collection of NFTs all housed within one single NFT.
    ‍
  • These can hold not only layers of value, but even deeper meaning as well. Empowering artists and creators. The only limit is your imagination.
    ‍
  • Collaborating in new ways, telling complex stories intertwined, all inside the NFT. Bringing new mechanics to the world of art, gaming, finance, the Metaverse, and much more.
Learn More

Use Case 03

Gaming

  • NFTs used as in-game items. The power of the in-game item could be dependent on the amount of "charge" the NFT holds.
    ‍
  • Charged Particles has the potential to eliminate the whole "Pay to Win" mechanic of gaming.
    ‍
  • Even a “Common” item can eventually build up enough power (via its charge) to compete with legendary items without paying for it.
Learn More

Use Case 04

Gift Basket

  • You can create an NFT and use it as a repository for your family members’ growing crypto stash.
    ‍
  • This could look like an NFT-based savings account for your child / family member.
    ‍
  • Whenever they’re ready, you can transfer the whole basket to them with a simple, single transaction.
